Cultural heritage is the shared legacy of a group of people, passed down from generation to generation. It encompasses tangible and intangible aspects, including art, music, literature, language, customs, traditions, and historical sites. These elements define a people's identity, values, and connection to their past. Cultural heritage is vital for fostering understanding, promoting diversity, and preserving the richness of human experiences.
